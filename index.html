Audio Delay WASM

Instructions:

  1. Open another tab and start the audio stream of the desired audio.
  2. Mute that tab. (right click on tab and select "mute site")
  3. Click "Start" in this tab and select the appropriate tab to share when prompted.
  4. (Optional) Cast this tab to a smart speaker.
  5. Adjust the delay to sync with your TV.
